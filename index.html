About the Conference

Welcome to the "Balancing AI Innovation and Duty" conference! This event aims to unite professionals from different fields to talk about how AI is evolving, its impact on society, and the importance of responsible development. Its goals are to inspire creativity, and make participants prepared to face the opportunities and risks that AI will bring in the years to come. Together, let's envision the future of AI!

Target Audience

AI Researchers, Tech entrepreneurs, Ai enthusiast, Business leader, Policymakers, Developers and Software engineers

Benefits

Provides a chance to make connections with AI professionals, explore new ideas, and equip us to know both the power and risks of AI to use it wisely.

Meet Our Speakers

Geoffrey Hinton

Godfather of AI

Geoffrey Hinton is a key figure in artificial intelligence, known for his work on neural networks and deep learning. He helped develop backpropagation, a method that allows AI to learn, leading to major advances in speech recognition and computer vision. He won the Turing Award in 2018 for his contributions to deep learning.

Timnit Gebru

Founder and Executive Director of Distributed AI Research Institute (DAIR)

Timnit Gebru is a researcher focused on AI ethics and bias. She co-led Google's Ethical AI team but left in 2020 after raising concerns about AI harms. Now, she leads the DAIR Institute, promoting fair and responsible AI.
